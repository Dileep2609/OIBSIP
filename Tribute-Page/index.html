Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was one of India's greatest scientists, visionaries, and leaders. He served as the 11th President of India and inspired millions through his dedication, humility, and contributions to science, technology, and education.

Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

Coming from a humble family, he worked hard throughout his life and became one of the most respected scientists in India.

He joined DRDO and ISRO where he played a key role in India's missile and space development programs.

Due to his remarkable contributions to missile technology, he became known as the "Missile Man of India".

Dr. Kalam inspired students and young innovators through his speeches, books, and vision for a developed India.
